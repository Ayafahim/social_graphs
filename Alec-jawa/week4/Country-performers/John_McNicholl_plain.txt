John McNicholl is a country singer from Northern Ireland.
McNicholl's first album, Something Old, Something New was released in 2004 and earned him the "Best Newcomer" award at the Irish World Awards.[1] He also represented Ireland at the CMA Global Showcase in Nashville. Since then, he has gone on to record a number of albums each earning him a following in both Ireland and the United Kingdom.[2]

Early life[edit]
He was born in Foreglen, a small village in Derry.[3]
