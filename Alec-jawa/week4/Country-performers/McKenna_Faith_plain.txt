McKenna Garcia (born January 6, 1995),  better known by her stage name McKenna Faith is a country music singer/songwriter from Ukiah, California.[1][2]


Career[edit]
On August 21, 2011 Faith opened the Sonoma Country Music BBQ in Santa Rosa, California, playing with Dierks Bentley, Thompson Square and Luke Bryan.[3] On February 14, 2012, McKenna Faith debuted her single "Head Over My Boots For You" to radio.[4][5] Faith performed on the Blake Shelton and Friends Cruise in 2013.[6]
On June 11, 2013 Faith released her first full length album, We Like Trucks.[7] That September, Faith performed at the Sonoma Stampede Country Music Festival. She performed with Frankie Ballard and Tate Stevens.[8]
