Redneck Souljers are an American country rap duo from Winchester, Tennessee, composed of rappers Wesley "Fatt Tarr" Alonso and Christopher "C-Hubb" Walls. They were formed in 2009 as a novelty act, parodying popular hits and remixing songs in a hick-hop style, then in 2013 they went "serious" with their debut Tiller Gang release.[2] Their first full-length studio album, Firewater, debuted on the Billboard charts.
