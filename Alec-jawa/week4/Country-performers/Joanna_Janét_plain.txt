Joanna Janét (born in Baton Rouge, Louisiana[1]) is an American country music singer. Janét was signed to DreamWorks Nashville and recorded an album for the label, Destination Love, that was scheduled to be released in August 2002.[2] The first single from the album, "Since I've Seen You Last," peaked at number 55 on the Billboard Hot Country Singles & Tracks chart.[1] When Janét's producer, Paul Worley, left DreamWorks for Warner Bros. Records, she went with him.[3]
