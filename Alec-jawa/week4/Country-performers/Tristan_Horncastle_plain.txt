Tristan Horncastle (born March 23, 1986)[1] is a Canadian country music singer-songwriter. Horncastle signed to Royalty Records in November 2013.[2] His first single for the label, "A Little Bit of Alright", was released in January 2014.[3] It peaked at number 34 on the Billboard Canada Country chart.[4] Horncastle's debut album, A Little Bit of Alright, was released via Royalty on May 27, 2014 and distributed by Sony Music Canada.[5]
