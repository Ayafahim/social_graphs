Ashley Cooke (born June 12, 1997) is an American country music singer-songwriter from Parkland, Florida signed to Big Loud.[1]


Background[edit]
Cooke independently released Already Drank That Beer - Side A in 2021.[2] The album was later retitled as Already Drank That Beer and reissued on April 1, 2022, adding a new duet version of "Never Til Now" which featured Brett Young. It was released as Cooke's debut single in May 2022 and promoted by Big Loud who she inked a recording contract with.[2] It peaked at number 49 on the Billboard Country Airplay chart. 
Shot in the Dark, Cooke's 24-track second studio and major label debut album, was released on July 21, 2023, via Big Loud.[3] It reprises Cooke's debut single "Never Til Now" and also features new collaborations with Colbie Caillat, Nate Smith, and Jackson Dean. "Your Place" was released on December 11, 2023, as the album's lead single,[4] and Cooke was the recipient of iHeartRadio's On the Verge program.[5]
