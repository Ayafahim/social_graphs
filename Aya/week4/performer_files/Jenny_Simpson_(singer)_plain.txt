Jenny Simpson (born in 1973 in Nashville, Tennessee[1]) is an American country music singer. She was signed to Mercury Nashville and released her self-titled debut album in 1998.[2]
Simpson's only single, "Ticket Out of Kansas", peaked at number 54 on the Billboard Hot Country Singles & Tracks chart.[1] It received a favorable review from Deborah Evans Price of Billboard, who said that "Simpson's nuanced vocal performance makes listeners feel as if they are boarding the bus with her."[3]
